  Tableau

You can use Tableau (included with your Kit of Parts) to help sort your data.

A starter file has be provided for you, which separates data by stage, incorporates some pit scouting attributes, and helps you get going with your analysis.

You may need to install a driver to connect to a PostgreSQL database.
