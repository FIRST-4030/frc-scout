  Tableau

You can use Tableau to access and analyze your data. Access your team's 5 license keys by having a mentor log onto TIMS and checking the virtual Kit of Parts.

A starter file has be provided for you, which separates data by stage, incorporates some pit scouting attributes, and helps you get going with your analysis.

These connector files will link Tableau with the most updated version of the database. You won't have to download this file again to get new data — Tableau will query it automatically. If your data is not updated automatically, go to the Data menu in Tableau, hover your mouse over the FRCScout.com source and click Refresh.

You may need to install a driver to connect to a PostgreSQL database.
