  What is FRC Scout?

FRC Scout is a web-based app that aims to make scouting and strategy much easier. Instead of frantically writing down robot attributes on paper, compiling everything manually, and attempting to form a strategy from that, FRC Scout will allow your team to collect data much more easily, and process it using Tableau (or other software of your choosing).

Each of your scouts can create an account, and then scout using our simple, intuitive interface. Our database is publicly accessible and updated in real time, so strategizing can be done more quickly.

Furthermore, you will not only have access to your own data, but also to that of other teams using FRC Scout. This helps every team by improving the overall quality of data. The more teams that use the app, the better it becomes!

  How does scout management work?

The first user that creates an account under your team's number will be treated as the team manager. Each subsequent scout will require approval from the team manager before they can log in and scout as a member of that team.

Of course, teams can have multiple managers and managers can be promoted or demoted by other managers.

  Who is behind all this?

FRC Team 4030 — NullPointerException has been developing scouting applications since Rebound Rumble. This year, we have teamed up with 1983 — Skunkworks to improve our data acquisition and processing, and to integrate our collection techniques with Tableau.

Both of us are from the Seattle, WA area.

  How do I get started?

You can sign up right here! If you are the first member of your team to create an account, congratulations, you will be the team manager! Otherwise, you will require approval from a manager before you may login.

We encourage you to look around and learn more, but please do not submit any data if you are not actually scouting a match! You may go through the scouting procedure and simply hit "Discard Data" at the end.

Arise, go forth, and conquer!
